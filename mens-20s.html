Style Vibes

Men's fashion guide for the digital generation 🔥

20s Edition

Streetwear

Urban culture meets high fashion. Bold graphics, oversized fits, and statement pieces that make you stand out. Perfect for concerts, skateparks, and city adventures.

🔥 Styling Tips for Streetwear

👟
Invest in statement sneakers - they're the foundation of streetwear
📏
Mix oversized and fitted pieces for balanced proportions
🎨
Layer graphic tees, hoodies, and jackets for depth
🧢
Add caps, beanies, or bucket hats to complete the look

Tech Minimal

Clean, functional, and futuristic. Inspired by tech bros and minimalist design principles. Perfect for startups, coffee shops, and modern city living.

💻 Styling Tips for Tech Minimal

🎯
Stick to neutral colors: black, white, gray, navy
📐
Focus on clean lines and perfect fits
🧵
Choose high-quality fabrics with technical features
⚡
Add one tech accessory like smartwatch or wireless earbuds

Alt Fashion

Dark, edgy, and expressive. Mix of goth, punk, and emo influences for those who want to stand out from the crowd. Perfect for music venues and creative spaces.

🖤 Styling Tips for Alt Fashion

🌙
Embrace black as your base, add pops of color sparingly
🔗
Mix textures: leather, denim, mesh, and metal
💀
Add statement accessories like chains or band pins
🎸
Layer band tees under mesh tops or open shirts

Smart Casual

Polished but relaxed. Perfect for dates, casual Fridays, and social events where you want to look put-together without being overdressed.

👔 Styling Tips for Smart Casual

🎯
Mix formal and casual pieces for balance
👞
Choose versatile shoes like loafers or clean sneakers
📏
Ensure everything fits well - tailoring makes a difference
⌚
Add subtle accessories like watches or simple chains

Y2K Revival

Nostalgic futurism meets early 2000s energy. Baggy jeans, metallic details, and tech-inspired pieces for a retro-futuristic vibe.

💿 Styling Tips for Y2K Revival

🌈
Mix bright colors with metallic accents
👖
Embrace baggy jeans and oversized silhouettes
🔮
Add tech accessories and futuristic details
🎮
Layer graphic tees with tech references

Vintage Workwear

Rugged, timeless, and practical. Inspired by blue-collar work clothes with a modern twist. Perfect for casual days and outdoor activities.

🔨 Styling Tips for Vintage Workwear

🍂
Stick to earthy tones: browns, greens, and blues
🧵
Choose durable fabrics like denim, canvas, and wool
👢
Invest in quality boots that can handle wear
📐
Focus on functional details and utilitarian design
