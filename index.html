Style Vibes

Your ultimate style guide for the digital age ✨

20s Edition

Trendy Minimal

Clean lines meet modern comfort. Perfect for the Instagram-worthy, effortless look that works from coffee shops to casual dates. Think elevated basics with a contemporary twist.

💫 Styling Tips for Trendy Minimal

🎨
Stick to a neutral palette with one pop of color
✨
Invest in quality basics that you can mix and match
📐
Focus on clean silhouettes and perfect fits
💎
Choose minimal jewelry in gold or silver

Soft Grunge

Edgy meets comfortable. This aesthetic combines the rebellious spirit of grunge with softer, more wearable pieces. Perfect for music festivals, art galleries, and expressing your creative side.

🎸 Styling Tips for Soft Grunge

🖤
Mix textures: denim, leather, velvet, and cotton
🌙
Layer pieces for depth and visual interest
💀
Add band tees, patches, or vintage graphic prints
⚡
Complete with chunky boots or worn sneakers

Indie Sleaze

Party-ready with an effortless edge. Think late-night concerts, art openings, and rooftop parties. This style embraces the glamorous mess of early 2000s indie culture.

🌟 Styling Tips for Indie Sleaze

✨
Mix metallic pieces with dark, moody tones
🕶️
Add statement sunglasses and bold accessories
🦓
Embrace animal prints and bold patterns
💄
Complete with smoky eyes and glossy lips

Dark Academia

Scholarly elegance with a mysterious twist. Perfect for library study sessions, campus life, and channeling your inner intellectual. Think timeless pieces with academic flair.

📚 Styling Tips for Dark Academia

🍂
Stick to autumn colors: burgundy, forest green, navy
📖
Layer blazers, cardigans, and vintage pieces
🕯️
Add vintage accessories like pocket watches
🎭
Choose structured, tailored silhouettes

Y2K Revival

Futuristic nostalgia meets modern trends. Embrace the playful, tech-inspired fashion of the early 2000s with a contemporary update. Perfect for standing out and having fun with fashion.

💿 Styling Tips for Y2K Revival

🌈
Mix bright colors with metallic accents
🔮
Add holographic or iridescent accessories
💎
Layer chunky jewelry and hair accessories
🦋
Play with fun prints and textures

Cottagecore

Romantic and whimsical with a touch of vintage charm. Perfect for farmers markets, picnics, and embracing a slower, more mindful lifestyle. Think floral prints and natural fabrics.

🌸 Styling Tips for Cottagecore

🌻
Choose natural fabrics like linen and cotton
🍄
Embrace floral prints and earthy tones
🧺
Add vintage accessories and wicker bags
🦋
Layer delicate jewelry and hair accessories
